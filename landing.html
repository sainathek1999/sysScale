The Framework

4 phases.
Every interview.

The same structure used at Google, Meta, Amazon, and every major tech interview. Practice it until it's automatic.

1
📋
Requirements
Clarify functional + non-functional requirements. State your assumptions. Define what's out of scope. Never skip this.
0–5 min
2
🔢
Capacity
Traffic → storage → bandwidth → node count. Show your math. Say real numbers. This is where most candidates lose points.
5–10 min
3
🏗️
Architecture
Draw the components, justify your data stores, explain the write and read paths. Name the tradeoffs you're accepting.
10–25 min
4
🔍
Deep Dive
Design decisions with options. Failure modes with mitigations. This is the phase that separates seniors from everyone else.
25–35 min
